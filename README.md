# ğŸ“Š Key Insights

Blue and Yellow products generated the highest sales.

Downhill subcategory is the top revenue driver among product types.

Size M products contributed significantly more revenue than Size L.

Sales in March were notably higher compared to February.

Credit Card is the most used payment method.

Air shipping slightly outperformed ground shipping in revenue.

Majority of orders were successfully Shipped, though some were cancelled.

# âœ… Recommendations

Increase stock and marketing focus on Blue and Downhill products.

Evaluate pricing or promotion strategy for lower-performing product colors and sizes.

Improve efficiency in order handling to reduce Processing and Cancelled orders.

Promote loyalty-based or cashback offers for Credit Card users since usage is high.
